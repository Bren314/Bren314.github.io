About Me

Dyslexic

I was diagnosed with Dyslexia when I was in kindergarten. This mainly affects my spelling and word recall abilities, however with many years of help from various programs I have improved both substantially. One method of helping my recall is repetition, verbally saying the same presentation 12+ times or the equation 30+ times is something I have done. Another method is incorporating a lot of movement, like putting post-its over my room. I always found my thoughts flowed better when I was walking, This may not work for everyone, but getting other senses to my studing such as writing with my finger on my arm can work to.  The key to this of course is to find what works for you so try a bit of everything.
